Overview

Purpose

This website will be a great place to talk about items spoken about our podcast, The 630 Remake. We speak about balancing spirituality and our ever-busy youthful, young adult lives. In this case, this website will operate as a marketing tool and as a blog for powerful relevant conversations regarding my experiences on my mission in Cape Town, South Africa. Engaging in life lessons that I have learned from the experience and inspiring listeners to become the best versions of themselves. Enter into your element.

Audience

I want this site to be a relatable and relevant platform for youth and young adults who are experiencing diminished faith. I want people to discover an interest in their spirituality and answer questions and create a community of people that honour Christlike attributes.
